Tech-Securing Call Script

Keep this open on every call until it's muscle memory. Goal: in 3–5 minutes find out if this tech can do this job, in our window, under our cap, professionally — and lock them.

Pre-Call Preparation
0
🔍 Source the Tech

You find your own techs — don't wait for a list. Search in this order and line up 3–5 numbers before you dial:

1
Yelp
Trade + city → 4.0★+ & Open Now → owner-operators
2
Google Maps
"[trade] near [city]" → good-reviewed ones
3
BBB
Accredited + owner-operated
4
Craigslist
Skilled Trades / Services section
5
Facebook
Marketplace services + local trade groups
🚫 Skip franchises (Roto-Rooter, Mr. Handyman, Glass Doctor…). We want independents.
The Call
1
👋 The Local Opening
"Hey, good morning/afternoon, I'm [Name]. Looking for a [trade] in [city]?"
  • DO NOT say "By the way"
  • DO NOT say "Hello, can you hear me?" or "How's your day?"
  • Only say the city — do NOT say the state right away
  • If asked how you found them: "Yeah, I recently moved into the neighborhood and am looking around..."
2
⚡ The Quick Pitch
"Cool. Quick one — [scope of work, 5–7 words]. Can you help?"
  • Keep scope description tight — 5 to 7 words max
  • Match the energy — casual, confident, brief
3
✅ Fast Vetting & Qualification
"When can you go — anything today?"
"Free estimate on site, right?"
If they agree → "Perfect, sending the address. Good number?"
  • Do not over-explain early
  • Get availability and estimate commitment first
4
🔒 Locking & Rules
"One last thing — you work independent, correct?"
"Cool, I'll text a couple house rules — quick read before you head out, sites are picky. Thanks man, text me anytime."
  • Confirm independence — critical qualifier
  • Frame rules as "site requirements" not your rules
Critical Warnings
⛔
OPSEC & Pricing — Hard Rules
1
NEVER name the client. "We are STEADYWRK Facilities Network. We coordinate maintenance for this site."
2
NO payment talk on the phone. "We pay standard ACH or check through our accounting platform. I'll text you the link."
3
NO on-site haggling. Approve quotes under NTE within 10 minutes.
4
Do NOT send the address until the Team Leader approves the tech.
Objection Handling
🔴 They Say 🟢 You Say
"Who is the company?" "We are STEADYWRK Facilities Network. We coordinate maintenance for this location."
"Do you pay via Cash App / PayPal / Zelle?" "We pay standard ACH direct deposit or check. Our texted brief has the setup link."
"How much does it pay?" "Depends on scope — you assess, quote, we approve, you're paid 5–7 days after. Solid techs get repeat volume."
"I need same-day payment." "We pay 5–7 business days after completion + photos — standard for all our jobs."
"I only work under my own brand." "On-site you present as part of our team — that's a hard rule. If it's a dealbreaker, no worries." → next number
"I'm booked today." "When's your soonest?" → fits the window? great. If not: "I'll keep your number for the next one." → next number
"Send me the address first." "I send full details once you're confirmed on our end — standard for us."
